Future Tools?

Sass is a software tool that helped move our craft forward, but not all "future tools" we might use are software.

Some great "future tools" are ideas about process, and even some old things we haven't used to the fullest.

Meetup had a problem

  • no central voice (copy or design)
  • you could tell which team built a feature
  • inconsistent UI components
  • lack of design consistency often left the users to do our job
  • horribly bloated CSS
  • we regularly hit the 288kb per-stylesheet IE limit*

*Meetup was already 10 years old in 2012
